Vince Mckelvie is an experimental artist based in Los Angeles. He specializes in computer generated animation and publishes his work as animated GIF loops, hardware accelerated interactive animation, and rendered real-time interactive animation.

First GIF?

It was a pixilation of me consuming a toy car and seeing it drive all around inside my body by drawing marker tracks on my arms, legs and head.

According to Mckelvie, GIFs are the polaroids of animation. They’re instant gratification, they’re simple but complex at the same time. For him it is also important to be able to display his art in a dynamic context.
