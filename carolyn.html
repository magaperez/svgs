Carolyn Frishling

3 words that best describe your work?

- Abstract | Biomorphic | Enigmatic -

Carolyn specializes in painting, drawing and printmaking. With time, she has expanded her art practice to include 2D digital works and videos.

This ultimately led her to create 3D digital images and turning them into physical sculptures. Her prints, videos and sculpture have been shown in galleries around the world.
